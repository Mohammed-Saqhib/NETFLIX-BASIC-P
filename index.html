Verify your TV when you're traveling or away from home

  1. Open Netflix on your TV
  2. When prompted with 'Device not part of household', select 'I'm Traveling'
  3. Choose to receive verification code via email or text
  4. Check your email/phone for the verification code
  5. Enter the code on your TV within 15 minutes
  6. Your TV will be verified for temporary use

Update your Netflix household to include this TV permanently

  1. From Netflix home screen on TV, press back button to open menu
  2. Navigate to 'Get Help' > 'Manage Netflix Household'
  3. Select 'Update Netflix Household'
  4. Choose 'Send Email' or 'Send Text' for verification
  5. Check your email/text for verification link
  6. Click 'Yes, This Was Me' in email or tap link in text
  7. Select 'Confirm Update' to complete the process

Netflix Household Rules

Basics

  • Netflix Household = devices on same internet connection
  • One account can have only one household
  • Devices automatically added when used on household network
  • Traveling exceptions available for temporary use

Limitations

  • 31-day limit for devices outside household
  • Must verify periodically when traveling
  • Extra member fees apply for permanent outside users
  • Some content may vary by location
